Honey Mustard Grilled Chicken

'If ye have faith as a grain of mustard seed', ye shall make and enjoy this simple, tangy, delicious grilled chicken dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Put a table spoon of oil on a medium heat pan
  2. Break directly in to the pan without whisking.
  3. Sprinkle salt and wait until cooked
